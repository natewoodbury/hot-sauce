ABOUT THE SAUCE

Thanks for being interested in hot sauce from Tumwater Pepperworks!

This sauce represents a love of hot and spicy things that was fed by friends and family. I enjoyed testing and tweaking this recipe and hope that you enjoy it as much as I do. This sauce is supposed to be used liberally on everything from your daily eggs to weekly burritos.

As I continue to build this project our goal is to begin selling online and support additional shipping and sauce options.
